## Why this blog exists

The Kuber Tech Blog is built to make useful technical topics easier to understand for business owners, marketing teams, and founders.

- Clear answers instead of jargon
- Faster publishing with markdown files
- A lightweight stack that keeps pages quick

## What readers can expect

We’ll publish practical posts around:

- Website performance and uptime
- Business email with custom domains
- Hosting and domain best practices
- Tools that help teams work better online
